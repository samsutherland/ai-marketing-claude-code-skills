## FAQ

**Do I need to be registered for Bowral?** No, anyone in the club can use the discount and train
along. If you are riding it, enter through the organiser.

**Which distance should I pick?** Whichever suits where you're at, 85, 120 or 150. Not sure? Start
the app and talk it through, or ask on a ride.

**Can I keep doing the normal Pastries rides?** Yes, they're part of the plan, not separate from it.

**What if I miss training?** That's the whole point. Streeka reworks the plan around the week you
actually had, not the one you hoped for.

**Is this an official Bowral Classic program?** No. It's a Pastries club training challenge. Official
event info comes from the organiser.
