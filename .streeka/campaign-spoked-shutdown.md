# Campaign — Spoked Shutdown (Switch to Streeka)

**Created:** 2026-06-23 · Status: build now, ship before **1 July 2026**
**Trigger:** Spoked (spoked.ai), a direct AI-coaching competitor, emailed customers that the
software is **discontinued as of 1 July 2026**. Their parting gift: 3 months free on **icTrainer.com**,
a €29.99/yr indoor-trainer control app (the email itself brags about streaming movies and
running two riders off one computer). That is not a coaching replacement.

## The opening (why this is rare)
Spoked's customers are, all at once:
1. **Forced to move** — their coach is being switched off.
2. **On a clock** — 8 days' notice.
3. **Pointed at a downgrade** — an indoor smart-trainer app, not an AI coach.

The cyclists who used Spoked to actually *get faster* — structure, outdoor riding, a coach that
thought with them — just got handed a garage app. They are shopping for a real replacement right
now, and searching for it. That is the entire Streeka ICP, mid-decision, with a deadline.

## The play
A **"Coming from Spoked?" switch campaign**, live before 1 July so it's indexed and circulating
while people search *Spoked alternative / Spoked shutting down / spoked.ai discontinued*.

**We win on substance, not price.** No special discount — standard 2-week free trial. The argument
is the product: Spoked's AI coach gave up; ours is here, you chat with it, and it tells you why.

## The wedge (lead with this, in order)
1. **It's a coach, not a trainer app.** icTrainer controls your turbo. Streeka coaches your training.
2. **You chat with it.** Ask "I missed Tuesday, what now?" in plain language — get an answer, not a dashboard.
3. **It explains every call.** When the plan changes, it tells you *why*. No black box (the live r/Velo fear).
4. **Your history isn't trapped.** Streeka reads what you actually rode straight from **Garmin or Wahoo** —
   connect once and it picks up your riding. Nothing to export out of Spoked.

## Migration reassurance (the practical objections)
- *"I'll lose my data."* → Your rides live in Garmin/Wahoo, not Spoked. Streeka reads them on connect.
- *"Setup hassle."* → Pick a goal, connect Garmin or Wahoo, start riding. 2-week trial, cancel anytime.
- *"Is it just another black box?"* → No — it explains every decision in plain language. That's the point.

- **1 July:** Spoked goes dark — switch any present-tense copy ("Spoked is shutting down") to past
  ("Spoked has shut down") so the page stays accurate and keeps ranking.
- **Through July:** let the page keep catching residual searches; refresh the founder post once.

## Guardrails (do not break)
- **"chat with", never "talk to / voice"** — SAMI is text chat only, no voice on iOS.
- **Never say "no data / no metrics"** — Streeka reads real Garmin/Wahoo data. Soft anti-metrics only;
  don't name TSS/CTL/ATL.
- **Garmin + Wahoo only. Never list Strava** as a Streeka integration.
- **Tone:** wry, not gloating. We're the steady hand for stranded riders, not dancing on a grave.
  Every claim about Spoked/icTrainer is fair because it's quoted from their own email.
- Anything that names another company stays factual and quotable — no invented features.
