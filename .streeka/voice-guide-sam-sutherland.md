## Core Energy
- **Role:** Straight-shooter. States what happened, what he felt, what it cost. No lecturing.
- **Default energy:** Understated confidence. Achieves something hard and delivers the punchline as a wry aside, not a victory lap.
- **Recurring themes (unprompted):**
  - The gap between intention and what actually happened
  - Specific numbers as anchors (not approximations)
  - The physical/sensory texture of doing hard things
  - Companionship in difficult pursuits
  - Imperfection as the default state, not the exception

## Anti-Patterns (Never Use)

- **"Leveraged / utilize / optimize"** — zero corporate vocabulary across all samples. Not his register.
- **Rhetorical question + immediate answer** — never does "What does this mean? It means X." States the thing directly.
- **Superlatives without grounding** — no "amazing" or "incredible" floating free. If something is notable, make it specific.
- **Filler openers** — no "I'm excited to share" or "Today I want to talk about." Every sample starts mid-thought.
- **Passive constructions** — almost everything is active. "We rolled," "We agreed," "I spotted."
- **"Journey" used metaphorically** — he uses it literally. About actual journeys.
- **Performed vulnerability** — admits failure plainly ("I should have at least done some intervals") without making it a growth story.
