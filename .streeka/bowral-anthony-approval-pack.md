## 1. The idea, in two lines

A **Road to Bowral** challenge for Pastries riders: pick your distance (85 / 120 / 150 km),
get an adaptive training plan that bends around club rides and real life, and ride to Bowral
on **18 October** fitter and better prepared, together, as a club. I handle the plan, the
content, and the support. It runs alongside Pastries; it doesn't try to replace it.

The hook is the challenge and the group, not a discount.

---

## 2. The offer

- **Pastries members:** 20% off Streeka for new sign-ups, right through to the event (~4 months).
- **Ride Leaders:** free access. They download Streeka, start a trial, and email me the line
  "I'm a Pastries ride leader and I've started a trial in Streeka", and I switch them to free.
  Kept between us, not mentioned in the member messaging. (Forwardable note in §7.)
- **One simple link does it all: streeka.com/bowral** (worth a look before Sunday). It covers how to
  claim on iPhone (one tap) or Android (download + message me), the ride dates, and a short FAQ.
- Riders pick the Bowral distance and goal that suits them, but no one has to be entered for
  Bowral, or even training, to take the discount. Anyone in the club is welcome to it.
