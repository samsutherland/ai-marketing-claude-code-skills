## THE PROBLEM

You have an event on the calendar. You're motivated. You're already tracking the numbers. You care about getting faster, keeping up, racing better — maybe dropping someone who has become a little too comfortable. Fair enough.

But you're also time poor. You have a job, a life, a Tuesday that falls apart. You need more than a static plan sitting there judging you when it does.

That's the gap. Most training apps give you a plan. None of them help you when the plan stops working.

---

## THE PROMISE

In 12 weeks, you will be ready for your chosen event.

Not hoping you did enough. Not guessing whether you trained well. Ready. You'll have worked through the block, adapted when life got messy, and arrived at your event knowing exactly what you put in.

---

## HOW IT WORKS

Two phases. One outcome.

**Phase 1: Initialise Plan**
You get your event plan and your first two weeks of structured training. The early win is simple: you stop guessing. You know what you're working toward, what the first step looks like, and how training fits around your actual life — not the pretend version where every week is clean and nobody gets tired.

**Phase 2: Complete Event**
You build toward your event with a plan that adapts when things change. Missed session, rough week, work blew out — the plan adjusts and you keep moving. By the end, you show up ready to ride hard and give yourself a real shot at the result you wanted.
